Do you like hiking or are you more of a couch potato? Allergic to pet dander and need a non-shedder? Live on a farm or in an apartment? With FurEver Home you can explore over 60 breeds of cats and dogs to find a breed that best fits your lifestyle.

You may wonder why this is important. Pets are often rescued from shelters or even bought from breeders only to be re-homed (sometimes more than once) because it turns out they aren't a good fit for their adopter's lifestyle. It is our hope that by using FurEver Home, users will become better informed about different breeds and can adopt more responsibly!
